Baked BBQ Chicken

Savor the Juicy and Tangy Delight of our Guilt-Free BBQ Chicken Recipe—Healthier Never Tasted So Good

Why YOU should try this recipe:

  1. Fast and convenient: Perfect for busy days, you can have a delicious dinner on the table in just 30 minutes.
  2. Simple and accessible: With minimal ingredients, most of which you likely have at home already, it's a breeze to prepare.
  3. Mouthwatering delight: Indulge in juicy, tender chicken generously coated in a flavorful sticky BBQ sauce that will leave you craving more.

Ingredients

  • 4 large chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup of BBQ sauce
  • Extra virgin olive oil / Avocado oil
  • Kosher salt
  • Ground pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat grill to high heat (450°F) for direct cooking. Clean the cooking grates.
  2. Drizzle chicken breasts with oil, season generously with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill chicken for 5-6 minutes, until they release easily from the grates. Flip and cook for another 4-5 minutes.
  4. Baste chicken with BBQ sauce, grill for 2 minutes on each side. Repeat on the other side.
  5. Cook until chicken is firm to the touch and reaches an internal temperature of 160°F (it will rise to 165°F while resting).
  6. Rest chicken for 5 minutes, covered with foil. Serve with additional BBQ sauce, if desired.
